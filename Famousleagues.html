Famous Leagues

Top 5 Cricket Leagues in the World

Indian Premier League

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a Twenty20 cricket league in India, with ten teams representing different cities. Its net worth is around $10.9 billion.

International League T20

The International League T20 (ILT20) is a Twenty20 cricket competition in the UAE, with 6 teams representing different cities. Its net worth is around $15 million.

SA20

The SA20 is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in South Africa, featuring 6 teams from across the country. Its net worth is around $12.5 million.

Big Bash League

The Big Bash League (BBL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in Australia, featuring 8 teams from across the country. Its net worth is around $10 million.

The Hundred

The Hundred is a professional cricket league in England and Wales, featuring 8 teams. Its net worth is around $9 million.
