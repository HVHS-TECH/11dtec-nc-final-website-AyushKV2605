Greatest Players

Profiles of top legends like Sachin Tendulkar, Don Bradman, and Sir Garfield Sobers.

Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is the highest run-scorer in international cricket, with over 34,000 runs across formats. He played for India from 1989 to 2012. He holds the record for the most centuries in international cricket (100).

Don Bradman

Sir Donald Bradman, often regarded as the greatest batsman of all time, had an astonishing Test batting average of 99.94. He played for Australia from 1928 to 1948.

Sir Garfield Sobers

Sir Garfield Sobers is widely regarded as the greatest all-rounder in cricket history. He played for West Indies from 1954 to 1974. He scored over 8,000 runs and took more than 200 wickets in Test cricket.
