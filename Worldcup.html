World Cups

The 3 main tournaments in cricket are the Cricket World Cup, the World Test Championship, and the T20 World Cup.

Cricket World Cup

The Cricket World Cup is the most important tournament in Cricket and is played in the One Day International (ODI) format. The tournamnet is organised by the International Cricket Council (ICC) and features teams from around the world. The cricket world cup is played on a 4 yearly basis.The First Cricket World Cup was held in 1975, and the inaugral trophy was won by West Indies.

World Test Championship

The World Test Championship is a tournament for Cricket teams that are in the Top 8 of ICC rankings In Test Cricket. This Final happens every two years The Inaugral WTC was held in 2021, and the first ever cup was won by New Zealand.

T20 World Cup

The T20 World Cup is a tournament for 20 cricket teams with the Top 8 teams in the rankings and other associate nations compete in this tournament. This torunament is featured in the 20-over format The T20 World cup happens every two years, which allows associate nations to play better teams. The first T20 World Cup was held in 2007, and the inaugural trophy was won by India.
